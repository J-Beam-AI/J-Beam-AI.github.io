About Jon Beam

I'm Jon Beam, an independent AI consultant helping small and owner/operator businesses cut through the hype and actually use artificial intelligence in ways that allows them to do the work they want to do. I am exploring ways where AI can benefit the trades (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, cleaning, roofing, and general contracting), helping turn everyday administrative tasks into practical AI-assisted tools.

My approach is simple: no jargon, no bloated projects, no solutions looking for problems. I start by listening, then design AI assisted results that fit your existing systems, your team's skill level, and your budget. Whether you need your first chatbot or an automation strategy, I deliver outcomes, not presentations.

Before going independent, I spent over 20 years in corporate, enterprise, and government environments, including seven years as a U.S. Army Signal Officer, managing technical teams and communications infrastructure across global locations. My background in workflow automation, process optimization, systems analysis, and change management provides the operational and technical foundation for my AI consulting solutions.

What I Do

End-to-end AI consulting tailored to small business realities — practical, affordable, and built to last.

Missed Call Recovery System

Deploy AI-powered answering systems that respond to missed calls within seconds via text, capturing caller information, and can book an appointment directly to your calendar.

Automated Quote Follow-Up

Build automations that contact a prospect at timed intervals after a quote has been sent — keeping your business top of mind without manual effort.

Automated Review Solicitation
